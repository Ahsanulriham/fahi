Assalamualaikum, I'm Fahima Akter

I am from Sylhet, Bangladesh, and currently studying Fashion Designing at Sonargaon University. My passion for fashion began at a young age, and now I’m developing my creative skills to turn that passion into a successful career.

Before attending university, I completed my schooling at UCEP, where I gained a strong foundation in various subjects. I love sketching, working with fabrics, and exploring new trends in design.

I'm creative, detail-oriented, and always eager to learn. I dream of launching my own fashion brand or becoming a professional designer to contribute to the global fashion industry.
